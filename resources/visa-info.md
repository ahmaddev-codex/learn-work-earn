# 🛂 Visa & Immigration Information

> Comprehensive visa and immigration information for UK, Canada, and Australia for students and software engineers.

---

## 🇬🇧 United Kingdom

### Student Visa (formerly Tier 4)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Who needs it** | All international students studying >6 months |
| **Duration** | Course duration + 4 months (Masters), +6 months (PhD) |
| **Cost** | £490 + Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS) |
| **IHS Cost** | £776/year (as of 2026) |
| **Processing Time** | 3 weeks (standard), 1 week (priority), 1 day (super priority) |
| **Work Rights** | 20 hrs/week during term, full-time during holidays |
| **Dependents** | Allowed for Masters/PhD students |

### Required Documents

- [ ] Valid passport
- [ ] CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies) from university
- [ ] Proof of funds: Tuition + £1,136/month (London) or £1,023/month (outside) for 9 months
- [ ] TB test certificate (if from listed country)
| - [ ] ATAS certificate (if required for course)
- [ ] English proficiency proof (if not from English-speaking country)
- [ ] Academic transcripts and certificates
- [ ] Parental consent (if under 18)

### Financial Evidence Requirements

| Location | Amount Required (9 months) |
|----------|---------------------------|
| **London** | Tuition + £10,224 (£1,136 × 9) |
| **Outside London** | Tuition + £9,207 (£1,023 × 9) |

**Funds must be in account for 28 consecutive days before application.**

### Graduate Visa (Post-Study Work)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Duration** | 2 years (Masters), 3 years (PhD) |
| **Eligibility** | Completed degree at UK university |
| **Cost** | £822 + IHS |
| **Work Rights** | Full-time, any employer, self-employment allowed |
| **Can switch to Skilled Worker** | Yes |
| **Settlement Path** | No direct path, but can switch to Skilled Worker |

### Skilled Worker Visa

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Duration** | Up to 5 years, renewable |
| **Cost** | £719-£1,639 (depending on duration) + IHS |
| **Salary Requirement** | £38,700/year or going rate (whichever is higher) |
| **Going Rate for Software Engineers** | £34,000-£45,000 |
| **Points Required** | 70 points |
| **English Requirement** | B1 level or degree taught in English |
| **Settlement Path** | Yes, after 5 years (Indefinite Leave to Remain) |

**Points Breakdown:**
| Factor | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Job offer from licensed sponsor | 20 |
| Job at appropriate skill level | 20 |
| English language skills | 10 |
| Salary £38,700-£48,100 | 0 |
| Salary £48,100-£62,400 | 10 |
| Salary £62,400+ | 20 |
| Job in shortage occupation | 20 |
| PhD in relevant subject | 10-20 |

### High Potential Individual (HPI) Visa

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Duration** | 2 years (3 years for PhD holders) |
| **Eligibility** | Graduated from top global university in last 5 years |
| **Cost** | £822 + IHS |
| **Work Rights** | Full-time, any employer, self-employment |
| **Eligible Universities** | Check UK government list (top 50 global) |
| **Settlement Path** | No, but can switch to Skilled Worker |

## 🇨🇦 Canada

### Study Permit

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Who needs it** | All international students studying >6 months |
| **Duration** | Course duration + 90 days |
| **Cost** | CAD $150 + CAD $85 (biometrics) |
| **Processing Time** | 4-12 weeks (varies by country) |
| **Work Rights** | 20 hrs/week during term, full-time during breaks |
| **Co-op Work Permit** | Separate permit required for co-op/internship |
| **Dependents** | Spouse can get open work permit; children can study |

### Required Documents

- [ ] Valid passport
- [ ] Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from DLI
- [ ] Provincial Attestation Letter (PAL)
- [ ] Proof of funds: Tuition + CAD $20,635/year living expenses (from Jan 2024)
- [ ] GIC (Guaranteed Investment Certificate) — CAD $20,635 (recommended)
- [ ] Medical exam (if from listed country or staying >6 months)
- [ ] Police clearance certificate
- [ ] English/French proficiency proof
- [ ] Statement of Purpose (study plan)
- [ ] Biometrics

### Financial Evidence Requirements

| Category | Amount Required |
|----------|----------------|
| **Tuition** | First year tuition fees |
| **Living Expenses** | CAD $20,635/year (outside Quebec) |
| **Living Expenses (Quebec)** | CAD $15,078/year |
| **Additional for Dependent** | CAD $4,000/year |
| **Additional for Each Child** | CAD $3,000/year |

### Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Duration** | 8 months-3 years (matches program length, max 3 years) |
| **Eligibility** | Completed program at DLI, full-time student status |
| **Cost** | CAD $255 |
| **Processing Time** | 4-12 weeks |
| **Work Rights** | Full-time, any employer, open work permit |
| **Can be extended** | No, but can apply for Bridging Open Work Permit |
| **Settlement Path** | Yes, Canadian work experience counts for Express Entry |

### Express Entry — Canadian Experience Class (CEC)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Eligibility** | 1 year Canadian skilled work experience, CLB 7+ |
| **Processing Time** | 6-8 months |
| **CRS Score Target** | 470+ (varies by draw) |
| **Job Offer** | Not required |
| **Settlement Path** | Yes, leads to Permanent Residency |

**CRS Points Breakdown:**
| Factor | Max Points |
|--------|-----------|
| Age (18-35) | 110 |
| Education | 150 |
| Language (English/French) | 160 |
| Canadian Work Experience | 80 |
| Foreign Work Experience | 50 |
| Canadian Education | 30 |
| Arranged Employment | 50-200 |
| Provincial Nomination | 600 |
| Sibling in Canada | 15 |
| French Language Skills | 50 |

### Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)

| Province | Program | Key Requirement | Processing Time |
|----------|---------|----------------|----------------|
| **Ontario** | OINP Tech Draw | Job offer in tech, CRS 400+ | 2-3 months |
| **BC** | BC PNP Tech | Job offer in tech | 2-3 months |
| **Alberta** | Alberta Tech | Job offer, lower CRS | 4-6 months |
| **Saskatchewan** | SINP Tech | Job offer, specific NOC | 3-4 months |
| **Manitoba** | MPNP Tech | Work experience in Manitoba | 4-6 months |
| **Nova Scotia** | NS Tech | Job offer, specific NOC | 3-4 months |

## 🇦🇺 Australia

### Student Visa (Subclass 500)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Who needs it** | All international students |
| **Duration** | Course duration + 2 months (Masters), +6 months (PhD) |
| **Cost** | AUD $1,620 |
| **Processing Time** | 4-8 weeks |
| **Work Rights** | 48 hrs/fortnight during term, unlimited during breaks |
| **Dependents** | Spouse can work 48 hrs/fortnight; children can study |

### Required Documents

- [ ] Valid passport
- [ ] Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from CRICOS-registered provider
- [ ] Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement
- [ ] Proof of funds: Tuition + AUD $24,505/year living expenses + travel |
- [ ] Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) — must purchase before visa |
- [ ] English proficiency proof (IELTS, PTE, TOEFL)
- [ ] Academic transcripts and certificates
- [ ] Health examination
- [ ] Police clearance certificate
- [ ] Biometrics (if requested)

### Financial Evidence Requirements

| Category | Amount Required (Annual) |
|----------|-------------------------|
| **Tuition** | First year tuition fees |
| **Living Expenses (Student)** | AUD $24,505 |
| **Living Expenses (Partner)** | AUD $8,574 |
| **Living Expenses (Child)** | AUD $3,670 |
| **Travel** | AUD $2,000-3,000 |

### Temporary Graduate Visa (Subclass 485)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|

## 📋 Visa Application Checklist

### Before Applying

- [ ] Research visa requirements for target country
- [ ] Check passport validity (must be valid for duration of stay + 6 months)
- [ ] Gather all required documents
- [ ] Arrange finances (28-90 days in advance)
- [ ] Book English test (if needed)
- [ ] Get medical exam (if required)
- [ ] Get police clearance (if required)
- [ ] Research health insurance requirements

### During Application

- [ ] Fill out application accurately
- [ ] Upload all required documents
- [ ] Pay application fees
- [ ] Book biometrics appointment (if required)
- [ ] Submit application
- [ ] Track application status online

### After Approval

- [ ] Receive visa/passport with visa
- [ ] Check visa conditions carefully
- [ ] Book flights
- [ ] Arrange accommodation
- [ ] Purchase health insurance
- [ ] Register with local authorities (if required)
- [ ] Open bank account
- [ ] Get local phone number

## 🚨 Common Visa Rejection Reasons

1. **Insufficient funds** — Most common reason
2. **Incorrect documents** — Wrong format, missing pages
3. **Fake documents** — Never submit fraudulent documents
4. **Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) failure** — Australia specifically
5. **English proficiency** — Not meeting minimum scores
6. **Health issues** — Failing medical exam
7. **Criminal record** — Failing police check
8. **Previous visa violations** — Overstaying, working illegally
9. **Inconsistent information** — Contradictions in application
10. **Late application** — Applying too close to program start

---

## 💡 Tips for Smooth Visa Processing

1. **Apply early** — At least 3 months before intended travel
2. **Be honest** — Never lie or omit information
3. **Be organized** — Keep all documents in one place
4. **Follow instructions exactly** — Word counts, formats, photo specs
5. **Keep copies** — Of everything you submit
6. **Track your application** — Use online tracking systems
7. **Don't book flights** — Until visa is approved
8. **Prepare for interview** — Some visas require interviews
9. **Have backup plan** — In case of delays or rejections
10. **Consult professional** — If complex case, consider immigration lawyer
