## 💡 Negotiation Tips

### Before Negotiating
1. **Research market rate** using this guide and Levels.fyi
2. **Know your BATNA** (Best Alternative to Negotiated Agreement)
3. **Prepare your case** - list your achievements and value
4. **Practice** - role-play with a friend

### What to Negotiate
| Component | Typical Range | Negotiable? |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Base Salary** | Fixed | ✅ Yes |
| **Signing Bonus** | $5k-50k | ✅ Yes |
| **Equity/Stock** | 0.01%-0.5% | ✅ Yes |
| **Relocation** | $5k-20k | ✅ Yes |
| **Annual Bonus** | 10%-30% of base | 🟡 Sometimes |
| **PTO/Vacation** | 20-30 days | 🟡 Sometimes |
| **Remote Work** | 0-100% | 🟡 Sometimes |
| **Professional Development** | $2k-5k/yr | ✅ Yes |
| **Equipment Budget** | $1k-3k | ✅ Yes |

### Negotiation Scripts

**For Base Salary:**
> "Based on my research and the value I can bring to this role, I was hoping for a base salary of [X]. Is there flexibility in the budget?"

**For Equity:**
> "I'm excited about the company's growth potential. Could we discuss the equity package? I was hoping for [X] shares/options."

**For Signing Bonus:**
> "Given that I'm relocating/leaving a bonus behind, would a signing bonus of [X] be possible?"
