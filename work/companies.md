## 🎯 Priority Matrix

### High Priority (Apply First)
- Companies that sponsor visas in your target country
- Companies with roles matching your exact skills
- Fast-growing companies with many openings
- Companies where you have connections

### Medium Priority (Apply Second)
- Companies with good culture but fewer openings
- Companies slightly above your current level
- Companies in your second-choice city

### Low Priority (Apply Last)
- Companies that don't sponsor visas
- Companies with very competitive hiring
- Companies in industries you're less interested in
