# 📝 Cover Letter Template

> Template for software engineering job applications across UK, Canada, Australia, and remote positions.

---

## 🎯 General Structure

1. **Header** - Your contact info + date + employer info
2. **Greeting** - Hiring manager or "Dear Hiring Manager"
3. **Opening** - Hook them with your enthusiasm + role
4. **Body Paragraph 1** - Why you're a great fit (skills + experience)
5. **Body Paragraph 2** - Why this company (research + alignment)
6. **Body Paragraph 3** - Key achievements (metrics + impact)
7. **Closing** - Call to action + availability
8. **Sign-off** - Professional closing

## ✅ Cover Letter Checklist

- [ ] Customized for the specific company and role
- [ ] Hiring manager's name used (if known)
- [ ] Company name spelled correctly
- [ ] Job title matches the posting exactly
- [ ] Specific achievements with metrics included
- [ ] Company research evident (mention recent news/products)
- [ ] No generic "To Whom It May Concern"
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Under 1 page (400-500 words max)
- [ ] Professional formatting and font
- [ ] Saved as PDF with clear filename: "YourName_CoverLetter_CompanyName.pdf"

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Generic letters** - Never use the same letter for multiple companies
2. **Repeating resume** - Don't just rehash your CV
3. **Too long** - Keep it under 1 page
4. **No company research** - Show you know about them
5. **Focusing on what you want** - Focus on what you can offer
6. **Typos** - Proofread multiple times
7. **Wrong company name** - Triple-check before sending
8. **No call to action** - End with clear next step
