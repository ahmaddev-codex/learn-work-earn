## 🎯 Tips for Different Countries

### UK Resume
- **Length:** 2 pages max for experienced, 1 page for new grads
- **Photo:** Not needed (and discouraged)
- **Personal info:** No age, marital status, or photo
- **Visa:** Mention right to work clearly
- **Spelling:** British English ("optimise", "colour", "centre")
- **Format:** Reverse chronological
- **References:** "Available upon request" or list 2

### Canada Resume
- **Length:** 2 pages max
- **Photo:** Not needed
- **Personal info:** No SIN, age, or photo
- **Visa:** Mention PR status or work permit
- **Spelling:** Canadian English (mix of British and American)
- **Format:** Reverse chronological
- **References:** "Available upon request"

### Australia Resume
- **Length:** 2-3 pages acceptable
- **Photo:** Optional (not common)
- **Personal info:** No age or marital status
- **Visa:** Mention visa status
- **Spelling:** Australian English (British-based)
- **Format:** Reverse chronological
- **References:** Often included (2-3 referees with contact info)

### Remote / US Resume
- **Length:** 1 page (<5 years exp), 2 pages (5+ years)
- **Photo:** Never include
- **Personal info:** Minimal (name, email, phone, city, links)
- **Format:** Clean, ATS-friendly
- **Keywords:** Match job description keywords exactly
- **Metrics:** Quantify everything possible
- **Action verbs:** Led, Built, Designed, Implemented, Reduced, Improved

## ✅ Resume Checklist

### Content
- [ ] Tailored for each job/program
- [ ] Keywords from job description included
- [ ] Every bullet has a metric or impact
- [ ] No "responsible for" — use action verbs
- [ ] No vague statements — be specific
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Consistent formatting throughout

### Formatting
- [ ] Clean, professional font (Arial, Calibri, Garamond)
- [ ] Font size 10-12pt for body, 14-16pt for headers
- [ ] Margins 0.5-1 inch
- [ ] Consistent bullet style
- [ ] Proper spacing between sections
- [ ] PDF format (not Word)
- [ ] File name: "YourName_Resume.pdf" or "YourName_CV.pdf"

### ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Friendly
- [ ] No tables, columns, or text boxes
- [ ] No headers/footers with important info
- [ ] No images, graphics, or icons
- [ ] Standard section headings ("Experience", "Education")
- [ ] Dates in consistent format (MM/YYYY or Month Year)

## ❌ Common Mistakes

1. **One-size-fits-all** — Never send the same resume to every job
2. **No metrics** — "Improved performance" → "Improved performance by 40%"
3. **Too long** — Recruiters spend 6-7 seconds on first scan
4. **Typos** — Use Grammarly, get a friend to proofread
5. **Outdated info** — Remove jobs from 10+ years ago (unless relevant)
6. **Irrelevant skills** — Don't list every technology you've touched
7. **Passive language** — "Was responsible for" → "Led", "Built", "Shipped"
8. **Missing links** — Always include GitHub, LinkedIn, portfolio
9. **Unprofessional email** — Use firstname.lastname@email.com
10. **No visa info** — For international applications, be clear about status
