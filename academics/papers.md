# 📄 Must-Read Papers & Reading List

> Essential papers to read before applying to graduate programs and reaching out to professors. Organized by research area.

## 🎯 How to Use This Reading List

1. **Focus on your top 2-3 research interests**
2. **Read 2-3 papers per professor** before contacting them
3. **Take notes** on key contributions, methods, and open questions
4. **Reference specific papers** in your cold emails and SOPs
5. **Track progress** using the status checkboxes
