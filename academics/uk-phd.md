### Important Notes on Funding
- **Contact the supervisor FIRST** — many PhD positions are funded via supervisor's grants
- **CDT programs** = 4-year fully funded routes including MSc in year 1 (excellent for direct PhDs)
- **EPSRC DTP:** International students get fewer funded spots — apply early
- **Self-funding:** PhD = 3-4 yrs × £30,000 = £90,000-£120,000; only if you have strong scholarship support

## 📝 Application Strategy — Step by Step

| Step | Action | Timeline | Priority |
|------|--------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Identify research areas and target supervisors | 12 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 2 | Read 3-5 papers per potential supervisor | 10 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 3 | Draft initial research proposal (1,000 words) | 9 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 4 | Email supervisors with CV + proposal | 9-12 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 5 | Refine proposal with supervisor feedback | 7-8 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 6 | Request 3 strong LORs from academic referees | 6 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 7 | Submit application by Dec deadline | Dec 2026 | 🔴 P1 |
| 8 | Apply for Gates/Clarendon/external scholarships | Oct-Dec 2026 | 🔴 P1 |
| 9 | Prepare for interviews | Jan 2027 | 🔴 P1 |

### Research Proposal Guidelines
- **Length:** 2,000–3,000 words
- **Sections:** Background, Research Questions, Methodology, Timeline, Expected Contributions, References
- **Tone:** Demonstrates you can think independently as a researcher
- **Tailoring:** Align with supervisor's current grants and interests
