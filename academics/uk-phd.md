### Key Funding Notes
- **Most UK PhDs are fully funded** for strong international students
- **Contact supervisors BEFORE applying** - funding often tied to specific projects
- **CDT programs** (Centre for Doctoral Training) offer structured 4-year programs with funding
- **Departmental scholarships** are competitive but available

## 📝 Application Strategy

### Critical Steps
1. **Identify supervisor** (6-12 months before deadline)
2. **Email potential supervisors** with CV and research interests
3. **Develop research proposal** (with supervisor input)
4. **Apply through university portal** (by deadline)
5. **Interview** (if shortlisted)
6. **Funding application** (separate or combined)

### Research Proposal Guidelines
- **Length:** 2,000-3,000 words
- **Include:** Research question, literature review, methodology, timeline, expected contributions
- **Tailor:** To supervisor's research interests and department strengths
