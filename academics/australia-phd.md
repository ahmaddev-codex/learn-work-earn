### Key Funding Notes
- **RTP (Research Training Program)** is the main PhD funding mechanism in Australia
- **All domestic students** receive RTP automatically
- **International students** compete for limited RTP places - apply early
- **Stipend:** AUD $32,000/yr (tax-free) + tuition covered
- **Top-up scholarships** available for high-achievers

## 📝 Application Strategy

### Australian PhD Structure
Unlike UK/Canada, Australian PhDs:
- **No formal coursework** (pure research from day 1)
- **Shorter duration** (3-4 years vs 4-5 in North America)
- **Rolling admissions** (multiple entry points)
- **RTP funding** is competitive for international students

### Documents Required
- [ ] Academic transcripts (all degrees)
- [ ] Honours/Masters thesis or research project evidence
- [ ] CV/Resume
- [ ] Research Proposal (1,500-2,000 words)
- [ ] 2-3 Letters of Recommendation
- [ ] IELTS/TOEFL score
- [ ] Publications (if any)
- [ ] Passport copy

### Important Notes
- **Contact supervisor first** - essential for Australian PhD applications
- **Research proposal** should align with supervisor's interests
- **RTP deadline:** Usually 3-4 months before intended start date
- **Multiple start dates:** Feb, July, and sometimes November

## 🏢 Immigration & Career

### Post-PhD Pathway
1. **PhD Completion** (3-4 years)
2. **Temporary Graduate Visa (485)** - 4 years for PhD graduates
3. **General Skilled Migration** → Apply for PR
4. **Permanent Residency** → Citizenship

### PhD Graduate Salaries
| Role | Salary Range (AUD) |
|------|-------------------|
| Postdoctoral Researcher | $70,000 - $95,000 |
| Research Scientist | $90,000 - $130,000 |
| Lecturer (Academic) | $100,000 - $150,000 |
