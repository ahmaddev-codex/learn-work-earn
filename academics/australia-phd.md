## 🌟 Why Australia for PhD?

- **RTP scholarship** = full tuition + AUD $32,192/yr stipend (tax-free) for research students
- **Shorter PhD** (3-4 years vs 4-5 in North America) → reach career earlier
- **Rolling admissions** → apply anytime; 3 intakes per year at most universities
- **4-year 485 visa** for PhD graduates → strong PR pathway
- **Points-based PR** rewards young, English-proficient graduates with Australian degrees

## 📝 Application Strategy — Australian PhD

### Key Differences from UK/Canada
- **No formal coursework** — research from Day 1
- **Supervisor agreement is essential** before applying
- **Rolling admissions** — apply anytime; 3 rounds/year at most unis
- **Research proposal** typically 1,500-2,000 words (shorter than UK/Canada)
- **Honours thesis** or Masters research project required as proof of research ability

### Step-by-Step Plan

| Step | Timeline | Priority |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. Identify research areas | 12 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 2. Read potential supervisors' papers | 10 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 3. **Email supervisor** — get provisional agreement | 9-12 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 4. Develop research proposal (aligned to supervisor's work) | 8 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 5. Request 2-3 LORs | 6 months before | 🔴 P1 |
| 6. Apply for RTP scholarship in same application | ASAP | 🔴 P1 |
| 7. Apply for Australia Awards (if eligible) | **2027-04-30** | 🔴 P1 |

### Research Proposal Guidelines (Australia)
- **Length:** 1,500–2,000 words (shorter than UK/Canada)
- **Sections:** Problem, Background, Methodology, Timeline, Expected Outcomes
- **Tone:** Show you understand the research landscape and can contribute
- **Align:** Closely match supervisor's current research projects / grants

## 🏢 Immigration Pathway After Australian PhD

| Stage | Visa | Duration | Notes |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| Study | Student Visa (500) | 3-4 years | Work rights: 48 hrs/fortnight during semester |
| Post-PhD Work | Temporary Graduate Visa (485) | **4 years** (PhD stream) | Full work rights, any employer |
| PR (Option 1) | Skilled Independent (189) | Permanent | Points-based; need 65+ points |
| PR (Option 2) | Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS 186) | Permanent | Employer-sponsored |
| PR (Option 3) | Skilled Nominated (190) | Permanent | State nomination + 5 extra points |
| Citizenship | Australian Citizen | Permanent | After 4 years (1 as PR) |

**PhD Score Calculation (General Skilled Migration):**
- Age 25-32: 30 points
- PhD (Australian): 20 points (education) + 5 points (Australian study)
- IELTS 8+ (Superior): 20 points
- 1-3 yrs Australian experience: 5 points
- **Estimated total: 75-80 points** — well above 65-point threshold
