## 💡 Tips for Reaching Out

1. **Read their recent papers first** - mention specific work in your email
2. **Be concise** - professors are busy, keep it to 3-4 paragraphs
3. **Show fit** - explain why their research aligns with your interests
4. **Attach CV** - always include a 2-page academic CV
5. **Follow up** - if no response in 2 weeks, send a polite follow-up
6. **Be patient** - response times vary from 1 day to 2 months
7. **Don't mass email** - customize each email individually
