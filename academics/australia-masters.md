## 📝 Application Documents Checklist

### Required for All
- [ ] Academic transcripts (all degrees)
- [ ] Degree certificates
- [ ] CV/Resume
- [ ] Statement of Purpose
- [ ] 2-3 Letters of Recommendation
- [ ] IELTS/TOEFL/PTE score
- [ ] Passport copy

### Australia-Specific
- [ ] Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) - issued after acceptance
- [ ] Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement
- [ ] Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) - must purchase before visa
- [ ] Financial evidence (tuition + living + travel)
- [ ] Student visa (Subclass 500) application

### Important Notes
- **No GRE** required for any Australian university
- **Multiple intakes:** Feb, July, and sometimes November
- **GTE requirement:** Must demonstrate genuine intent to study and return home
- **OSHC mandatory:** Must have health insurance for entire study period
- **Work rights:** 20 hrs/week during term, unlimited during breaks

### Immigration Pathway
1. **Student Visa (500)** → Study (2 years)
2. **Temporary Graduate Visa (485)** → Work (2-4 years depending on degree)
3. **General Skilled Migration** → Apply for PR
4. **Permanent Residency** → Citizenship

**Key Advantage:** Longer post-study work rights (2-4 years). Points-based PR system favors young, skilled graduates.
