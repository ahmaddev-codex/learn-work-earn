## 📝 Application Documents Checklist

### Required for All
- [ ] Academic transcripts (all semesters)
- [ ] Degree certificate / provisional
- [ ] CV (2 pages, academic format)
- [ ] Statement of Purpose (tailored per university)
- [ ] 2-3 Letters of Recommendation
- [ ] IELTS UKVI score (not just Academic!)
- [ ] Passport copy

### Oxford/Cambridge Only
- [ ] Writing sample or research proposal
- [ ] Detailed academic references

### Important Notes
- **IELTS UKVI required** for visa (not just IELTS Academic)
- Book IELTS UKVI early - centers fill up 4-6 weeks in advance
- **Financial evidence:** Funds must be in account for 28 days before visa application
- **No GRE** needed for any UK university
