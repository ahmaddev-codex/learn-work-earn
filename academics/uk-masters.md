## 📋 Application Documents Checklist

### Required for All UK Universities
- [ ] Academic transcripts (all semesters) — certified/attested
- [ ] Degree certificate or provisional certificate
- [ ] CV — 2 pages max, academic format
- [ ] Statement of Purpose (SOP) — tailored per university (500-1,000 words)
- [ ] 2–3 Letters of Recommendation (academic referees preferred)
- [ ] **IELTS UKVI** score (not just Academic — needed for Student Visa)
- [ ] Passport copy (biographic page)

### Oxford / Cambridge Only
- [ ] Detailed academic references from 2 referees
- [ ] Writing sample or research proposal (PhD)
- [ ] Supplementary application form (some colleges)

### Important Notes
- **IELTS UKVI vs IELTS Academic:** UK visa requires UKVI. Take the right test.
- **Financial evidence:** Funds must be in bank account for 28 consecutive days before Student Visa application.
- **No GRE:** Not required at any UK university.
- **Rolling admissions:** Apply in October–November, not March — earlier = higher scholarship chance.
